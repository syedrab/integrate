Our Services

IBI (Intensive Behavioural Intervention)

IBI applies the principles of ABA in an intensive and highly structured setting, approximately 20-40 hours of therapy per week.

ABA (Applied Behaviour Analysis)

A treatment method clinically proven to help individuals learn skills and make positive changes in behaviour.

Counselling

Provides individuals with an opportunity to share their feelings, develop positive coping strategies and learn new skills.

Occupational Therapy

Helps individuals learn, re-gain and develop the necessary life skills required to live a meaningful, independent and functional life.

Group Programs, Workshops, Camps

A range of educational, skill-building and supportive group programs for children, adolescents and adults.

Parent/Guardian Support

Programs aimed to assist parents/guardians in developing skills to promote a positive parent-child relationship and to manage behavioural concerns.

Speech and Language Therapy

Services to assess and treat speech concerns and help individuals communicate more effectively.

Education Services

Comprehensive assessments of a child's educational strengths and challenges and treatment options to help students reach their academic goals.

Psychological Assessments

A range of brief and comprehensive assessment services using standardized measures to determine a potential diagnosis and/or to provide recommendations.

Naturopathic Services

A primary health care model that blends modern science with traditional and natural forms of healing.

Professional Development

Dynamic staff training workshops customized to meet workplace needs and to facilitate learning and skill development.

Art Therapy

A form of psychotherapy that encourages free self-expression, through various creative formats.

What Our clients Say About Us

"I was getting phone calls daily from the school about my 6 year old son’s disruptive behaviour. I was a ball of anxiety, and didn’t know what to do. The Behaviour Therapist came to school meetings with me to be sure the school was implementing their suggestions, and to facilitate a less stressful, smooth, and consistent daily routine. My son also received occupational therapy to strengthen his fine motor skills, and to build his self-esteem and confidence. With their help, he got over that hurdle, and he is now thriving in school. We would not be where we are today without the compassionate, caring, helpful guidance and support of the professionals at this clinic."
