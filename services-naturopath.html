Naturopathic Services

What is Naturopathic Medicine?

Naturopathic Medicine is a primary health care model that blends modern science with traditional and natural forms of healing.  We believe in addressing all co-existing health concerns, as to treat the whole person with the aim of identifying a common underlying cause. Our passion lies in treating imbalances to health, rather than waiting for the body to enter a diseased state before healing.

Naturopathic doctors have a variety of natural healing modalities in our treatment toolboxes.  These modalities include, but are not limited to Botanical medicine, Clinical Nutrition, Orthomolecular medicine (vitamins, minerals and natural supplements), Homeopathy, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), acupuncture and lifestyle counselling.

 


Why would I access this service?

We are primary health care practitioners and are highly trained to support any condition you would normally seek a family physician’s medical advice for. We are also experts in determining when a natural versus a conventional medical approach is indicated for best care and will refer if the concern is beyond our scope of practice in the same way a family physician would refer to a specialist when appropriate.

At Integrate Health Services and the Kids Clinic, we provide care to the whole family; however, we have a particular focus in treating children with behaviour and mental health concerns including but not limited to:

  • Autism spectrum support
  • Attention (hyperactivity) deficit disorder
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Body image issues
  • Anger management and temper tantrums
  • Eating disorders
  • Smoking and addiction cessation support
  • Nutrition, lifestyle and exercise counselling

 

Other pediatric conditions seen:

  • Skin concerns – eczema, acne
  • Digestive concerns – IBS, constipation/diarrhea, food intolerance
  • Immune support – cold and flu management, strengthen resistance to future infections
  • Infant concerns – colic, food introduction, cradle cap, allergy and asthma prevention, etc.

 

What are the benefits of Naturopathic Services?

  • Decrease fatigue and enhance energy
  • Improve brain fog and improve focus
  • Alleviate joint and muscle pain
  • Balance hormones - resolving menopausal symptoms, fibroids, menstrual cramps, PCOS, endometriosis, heavy bleeding, hormonal headaches, etc
  • Resolve skin conditions - acne, eczema, psoriasis
  • Support mental health particularly anxiety and depression
  • Improve sleep quality and quantity 
  • Manage gastrointestinal concerns - not limited to constipation, diarrhea, Crohn's, Ulcerative colitis, IBS, SIBO and heart burn)
  • Optimize the cardiovascular system - addressing high blood pressure and elevated cholesterol
  • Balance blood sugar in type I and type II diabetes
  • Modulate the immune system for allergies, autoimmune conditions and frequent colds and flus
  • Support the thyroid for maintained hormone balance
  • Enhance natural libido
  • Achieve sustainable weight loss
  • Optimize fertility, pre- and post- natal care
